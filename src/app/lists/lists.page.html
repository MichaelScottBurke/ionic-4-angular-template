Lists

Lists are a continuous group of related or like items with a limited amount of content. They are composed of items containing primary and secondary information and actions, represented by icons and/or text.

Types

Text, Avatar, Icon; 1-line, 2-line 3-line; View, Navigation, Selection. Any of the following list types can be either a view, navigation or selection list (examples are shown as navigation lists).
