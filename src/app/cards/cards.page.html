Cards

Cards contain content and actions about a single subject. They should be easy to scan for relevant and actionable information. Elements, like text and images, should be placed on them in a way that clearly indicates hierarchy.

Use cards instead of lists when items have more than three elements of meta information or when visual interest and variety is important.

Examples

Cards are very flexible and composable components that can host a variety of atoms.
