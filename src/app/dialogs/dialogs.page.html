Alert dialogs

Alert dialogs interrupt users with urgent information, details, or actions. To close, an action must be selected.

Simple dialogs

Simple dialogs display items that are immediately actionable when selected. They don’t have text buttons. Alternatively, use menus for contextual iunformation or select (dropdown) menus for less disruption for simple lists of choices.

Confirmation dialogs require users to confirm a choice before the dialog is dismissed.

Full-screen dialogs
